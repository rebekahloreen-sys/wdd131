Common Questions

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

We recommend placing your order at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially for large or complex designs.

Do you deliver cakes?

Yes! We offer delivery within a 20-mile radius for an additional fee.

Can I request a fully custom design?

Absolutely. You can submit inspiration photos or describe your idea, and we’ll create something unique.

Do you accommodate allergies?

We offer gluten-free, dairy-free, and nut-free options upon request, but we are not an allergen-free kitchen.

What size cakes do you offer?

We provide single-layer, double-layer, sheet cakes, and tiered cakes for events of all sizes.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 50% deposit is required for all custom orders to secure your date.
